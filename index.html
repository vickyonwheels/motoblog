About Me

I’m Vicky, a rider who captures the raw, unfiltered thrill of two wheels on my Royal Enfield GT650. From cruising empty highways in drizzle to battling Chennai traffic chaos or chasing riders in the hills, my rides are never the same. I share real motovlogs, twin-cylinder symphonies, and the unexpected adventures Indian roads throw at me—sometimes peaceful, sometimes hilarious, always worth it. Riding isn’t just travel—it’s therapy, adrenaline, and storytelling on wheels.
